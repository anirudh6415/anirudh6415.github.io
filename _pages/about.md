As a `Robotics and Autonomous Systems in Artificial Intelligence graduate student at Arizona State University`, I bring a unique perspective to the field of engineering. While my formal education began with a degree in `Computer Science and Engineering from Dayananda Sagar University (DSU) in Bengaluru`, I initially pursued an internship in the `Centre for Artificial Intelligence & Robotics Lab (DRDO)` in the same city. However, after a brief stint there, I found my true calling in handling data, which I explored further in collaboration with my professor, Dr. Rajesh T.M at DSU.

Unfortunately, my plans were temporarily derailed due to the COVID-19 pandemic. I had to take over my family's bakery business and worked hard to turn it into a successful enterprise. I analyzed the bakery's sales data from the past two years and identified which products were most popular. With the goal of providing fresh and delicious baked goods, I developed a slogan, `"Make Fresh Sell Fresh,"` which resonated with customers. I also implemented new packaging techniques that helped increase `my profits`.

Despite the success of my bakery business, my true passion lay in the field of image processing and computer vision. With this in mind, I decided to enroll at Arizona State University to further my knowledge in machine learning.

These diverse experiences have taught me an invaluable lesson - that when you love what you do, you can achieve great things. I am excited to continue exploring my passion for engineering and AI and look forward to the many opportunities that lie ahead.
